Who & What

The DataTank is a RESTful data mangement system written in PHP5. It's ready to be your customised datahub inside your organisation. The software is managed by OKFN Belgium.

A professional service is guaranteed by our core team of developers. To help us lifting The DataTank to the next level, join our user group of enthiusiasts who meet every two months to decide the next priorities of The DataTank development.
